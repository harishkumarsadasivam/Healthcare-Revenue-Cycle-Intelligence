# Healthcare Revenue Cycle Intelligence Platform

## Project Objective

Develop an end-to-end healthcare analytics platform that identifies revenue cycle risks caused by documentation delays, coding delays, charge lag, and unresolved encounters.

The platform will provide operational and financial insights through SQL analytics, Python ETL pipelines, and Power BI dashboards.

---

## Business Problem

Healthcare organizations lose revenue when encounters remain incomplete due to missing documentation, delayed coding, provider attestations, or charge posting delays.

Current reporting often identifies issues after significant aging has occurred.

This platform aims to proactively identify at-risk encounters and support operational decision-making.
